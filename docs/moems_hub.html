🎲 Housie (Math Bingo)

30 unique-answer questions, 12 student cards, first to complete a row or full house wins. Open the teacher version on the projector and have each student open the student version on their device.

👩‍🏫 Housie — Teacher Console Teacher
Show questions one by one · Peek answers privately · Validate BINGO claims against numbers called.
🃏 Housie — Student Card Student
Pick your name · See your unique card · Tap cells to mark when you have the answer.
🎲 Housie — All-in-One Combined
Original single-screen version with questions, all 10 cards, and auto-tracked winners. Good for solo demo or small groups.

🏆 Team Challenges

Multi-question team rounds modeled after MOEMS Division M. Each round has its own question set, different difficulty curve, and grading flow.

📘 Round 1 — Team Challenge Combined
Original team round (5 questions, 30-min timer, mixed topics).
📗 Round 2 — Two-Team Grading Teacher
Two teams compete · Teacher grades each question after both teams submit answers. Per-question control.
📙 Round 3 — Student (30 min) Student
10 self-paced problems, easy → hard. Self-checked at the end. 30-minute stretch round.
📙 Round 3 — Teacher (per-Q grade) Teacher
Same 10 problems, teacher-driven · type student answers, grade live, see solutions.
