📋 Teacher Flow

  1. Make sure each student has the student cards page open on their device.
  2. Click "▶ Show next question". Read aloud, give students time to solve and self-mark on their card.
  3. If you need to verify the answer privately, click "👁 Show" on the dashed yellow box (only visible on your screen). Click 🙈 Hide to conceal it again. Do not project the answer — students should solve it themselves.
  4. Click "▶ Next question" to continue.
  5. When a student shouts BINGO (top row / middle row / bottom row / full house):
    • Pause the game.
    • Have them read out the numbers they marked for that line (5 numbers for a row, 15 for full house).
    • Type/paste those numbers into the "Validate BINGO claim" box on the right.
    • Click Validate. The system checks every number against the answers called so far.
  6. If all numbers are valid (already called) and the count matches the line type → 🎉 legit BINGO. Otherwise → false claim.
