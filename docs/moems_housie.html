📋 How to Play (Indian Housie / Math Bingo)

  1. Assign cards. Each of the 10 cards below is unique (15 random answers from a pool of 30). Type each player's name into the box at the top of a card to assign it.
  2. Click "▶ Show next question" to display a math question on the big purple panel above. Everyone solves on paper.
  3. Players check their card. If the answer to the question appears anywhere on their card, they call out and tap that cell to mark it. (You as teacher can also tap cells if you're managing for younger kids.)
  4. Click "Reveal answer" on the question panel after the room has had time, then move on to the next question.
  5. Prizes (the system tracks them automatically):
    • 🥇 First to complete the top row (5 cells, yellow row)
    • 🥈 First to complete the middle row (5 cells, blue row)
    • 🥉 First to complete the bottom row (5 cells, pink row)
    • 🏆 First to complete the full house (all 15 cells)
  6. Coaching tip: Verify each player's claim by checking their card and the called numbers (visible on the right). Don't auto-trust — Housie etiquette says false claims forfeit the prize!
